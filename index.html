Workflow

Four stages that move from product idea to durable ad-supported release.

We like production systems that are easy to explain. The workflow below is how a studio keeps iOS development, IAA monetization, and public-facing trust signals moving in the same direction.

01. Product framing

Clarify the product promise, session shape, release scope, and app-store-facing story before build momentum starts.

02. Core build

Implement the iOS app or game loop with platform fit, onboarding clarity, and strong interaction pacing.

03. IAA revenue architecture

Design ad moments, mediation readiness, reward logic, and experience guardrails around retention instead of disruption.

04. Public support layer

Publish support, privacy, and contact pages that make the product look stable to users, platforms, and partners.
