Studio Profile

About a studio that thinks in products, revenue loops, and public trust.

SingularityLaber exists for mobile software that needs a stronger operating model. We build iOS products, shape advertising-supported user flows, and keep the visible website layer clear enough for users, partners, and stores to understand the business behind the app.

Studio Position

Not a giant agency. Not a disposable microsite shop.

We sit closer to the product itself. That means tighter release scope, more attention to App Store fit, and better coordination between the app, the monetization system, and the public pages attached to it.

product clarity iOS-first delivery IAA discipline visible support
Studio Principles

What we optimize for before the launch page ever goes live.

Every release decision affects later support, store review, and monetization performance. The studio focuses on the choices that keep those layers from contradicting each other.

Principle 01

Readable product promise

Users should understand what the app is for quickly, whether it is a utility tool or a compact game loop.

Principle 02

Monetization without chaos

IAA revenue works better when the placement logic is deliberate, documented, and tied to actual session flow.

Principle 03

Public pages that feel maintained

Support, privacy, and business contacts should look intentional because they signal legitimacy.

Operating Lanes

Three lanes define how the studio works.

  • Build lane iOS app development and lightweight iOS game implementation, with a focus on concise scope and faster release readiness.
  • Revenue lane IAA monetization planning, placement design, frequency logic, reward moments, and experiment structure.
  • Visibility lane Public websites, privacy pages, support flows, and contact surfaces that support the product commercially.
Who We Work With

Best fit for product owners who need more than engineering.

  • Publishers with ad-supported titles Businesses that need better IAA pacing, support structure, and privacy coverage.
  • Commercial partners Clients who need visible contact routing and stable project communication after launch.
